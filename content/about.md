# About SewSonia

My goal is to help you feel and look your best with personalized bridal and formalwear alterations. As a dedicated seamstress with a passion for craftsmanship and detail, I specialize in creating fit-for-you tailored gowns, and personalized alterations that celebrate your unique style and silhouette.

## My Story

It all started with a handmade skirt and a little fashion show competition at 10 years old to start my love for sewing garments. Now 25 years later I’ve become obsessed with ball gowns. I found bridal wear to be the most fantastic. Something about making someone feel that princess moment on one of the most magical days of their life is so special..
