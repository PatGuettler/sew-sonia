# Services

At Elegant Stitches by Sonia, I offer a comprehensive range of services to ensure you look and feel your absolute best for your special day or formal event.

## Custom Bridal Gowns

Transform your wedding dress dreams into reality with a completely custom-designed gown tailored to your unique style, body shape, and wedding theme.

- **Full Custom Design**: From concept to creation, a dress designed specifically for you
- **Semi-Custom Design**: Modify existing designs to create your perfect look
- **Timeline**: 6-9 months recommended for custom gowns
- **Process**: Includes concept consultation, fabric selection, multiple fittings, and final detailing
- **Pricing**: Starting at $1,800, varies based on design complexity and materials

## Bridal Alterations

Expert alterations to make your purchased wedding dress fit you perfectly.

- **Comprehensive Alterations**: Including bodice adjustments, hem alterations, bustle addition, and structural changes
- **Minor Alterations**: Strap adjustments, simple hems, and minor fitting issues
- **Timeline**: 2-3 months before wedding date recommended
- **Pricing**: Starting at $350, based on assessment of required alterations

## Formalwear & Special Occasion

Custom-designed dresses and alterations for all your special events.

- **Custom Formal Dresses**: Evening gowns, prom dresses, mother-of-the-bride/groom dresses
- **Formal Menswear**: Custom vests, ties, and accessories to coordinate with bridal party
- **Alterations**: Expert alterations on store-bought formal attire
- **Timeline**: 3-4 months recommended for custom pieces
- **Pricing**: Starting at $450 for custom pieces, alteration pricing based on assessment

## Bridal Party Services

Ensure your entire bridal party looks cohesive and feels comfortable.

- **Bridesmaid Dresses**: Custom designs or alterations
- **Flower Girl Dresses**: Custom creation or modifications
- **Coordinating Accessories**: Custom veils, sashes, ties, and pocket squares
- **Group Booking**: Special pricing available for full bridal party services
- **Timeline**: 3-4 months recommended
- **Pricing**: Varies based on services required

## Consultations

Schedule a consultation to discuss your vision and explore how I can help create your perfect look.

- **Initial Consultation**: 60-minute appointment to discuss needs and options
- **Design Consultation**: For custom pieces, includes sketching and fabric selection
- **Pricing**: $75 initial consultation fee (applied to final service cost when booked)

To schedule a consultation or inquire about services, please [contact me](/contact/).
